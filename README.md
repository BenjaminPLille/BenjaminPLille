# Benjamin Podvin | PharmD

### Assistant Professor in Hematology • Clinical Laboratory Scientist • CHU Lille • University of Lille

I am a clinical laboratory scientist and researcher specializing in hematology, with a particular interest in multiple myeloma, cancer genomics, immunotherapy, and translational bioinformatics.

My research focuses on integrating genomic, transcriptomic, proteomic and clinical data to better understand disease biology, identify biomarkers, and improve precision medicine in hematologic malignancies.

# Current research

### Multiple myeloma

Integrative multi-omics analyses to characterize disease biology, treatment response and mechanisms of resistance to BCMA-directed immunotherapies.

### Cancer genomics

Analysis of somatic mutations, copy-number alterations, structural variants, breakpoint junctions and clonal evolution using next-generation sequencing.

### Single-cell technologies

Development of analytical workflows for single-cell RNA sequencing and single-cell DNA sequencing to investigate tumor heterogeneity and therapeutic resistance.

### Translational bioinformatics

Development of reproducible computational pipelines for genomic, transcriptomic and proteomic analyses in clinical hematology.
